Support

Need help with TI Timer? We are happy to assist with bugs, feedback, and feature requests for your board-game nights.

Contact

Email us at titimerapp@gmail.com.

We typically respond within a few business days.

Common topics

  • Turn timing — timers use real timestamps and should stay accurate when the app is backgrounded or your phone is locked.
  • Live Activities — enable notifications for TI Timer in iOS Settings if the Lock Screen timer or Next Player button is missing.
  • iCloud backup — sign in to iCloud on your device and enable iCloud for TI Timer in Settings to sync players and game history.
  • Player voices — recorded and AI-generated announcements stay on your device unless you use iCloud backup.
  • Crash recovery — if a long game was interrupted, reopen the app; TI Timer attempts to restore in-progress sessions automatically.

Report an issue

When contacting support, please include:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The TI Timer app version (Settings → About in the app, if available)
  • Whether you were using Live Activities, iCloud, or TI4-specific features
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots, if helpful
