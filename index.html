TI Timer

A turn timer built for long board-game nights.

Especially Twilight Imperium 4—but it works for any game where you want to know exactly how long each player has been thinking.

Start a game with your group, tap Next Player as turns pass, and TI Timer tracks every turn, round, and pause automatically. Timers use real timestamps, so they stay accurate even if you background the app, lock your phone, or play for hours.

Fast to use, accurate over 12-hour games, and designed for players who care about pace as much as strategy.

Built for TI4

Track strategy cards, initiative, trade goods, and round flow with tools tailored to Twilight Imperium. Mark cards as used, manage passes, and review detailed stats when the game ends.

Made for the table

A large, readable active-player display works in portrait and landscape. Live Activities put the current player, turn timer, and next player on your Lock Screen—with a Next Player button so you can advance turns without unlocking your phone.

Your crew, your voices

Save players to a library with custom colors. Record name announcements or generate them with AI, then hear “Krista… it’s your turn” when someone’s up.

Stats that matter

See live thinking-time stats during play and full results afterward—rankings, charts, group statistics, and a TI4 Hall of Fame for your regular group.

Reliable over long sessions

Undo mistakes, recover after a crash, and back up players and game history to iCloud so a reinstall doesn’t wipe your group’s data.
